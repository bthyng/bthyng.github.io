What is this all about?

Day By Day is an effort in self care. Upon entering grad school, enduring a breakup, and moving 13 hours from where I called home; I found myself forgetting to eat or drink water throughout the day. To give myself a gentle reminder to take care of myself I carry a little card to track my progress.

The front of the card asks the same thing everyday:

  • Did you eat today?
  • Did you drink H2o?
  • How are you feeling today?
  • Goal for the day?

And the back is a place for me to reflect what I am thankful for that day.

This is a space for the cards and the things I am thankful for to live after they are completed each day.
